# Mystery Match Classic 🀄

**Mystery Match Classic** is a beautifully crafted Mahjong Solitaire game with a mysterious twist. Challenge your mind by matching identical tiles, clearing the board layer by layer, and beating the clock. Featuring a classic pyramid layout, smooth animations, and helpful gameplay features, it's the perfect puzzle experience for both beginners and solitaire enthusiasts.

##  How to Play

1. **Objective** – Remove all tiles from the board by matching identical pairs.
2. **Free tiles** – A tile is free if:
   - No tile is directly on top of it.
   - It is not blocked on **both** left and right sides by other tiles on the same layer.
3. **Matching rules**:
   - Two tiles can be matched if they have the same **suit and rank** (e.g., two 5‑Dots).
   - Flowers match with any other flower, seasons match with any other season (they are considered identical groups).
4. **Controls**:
   - Click a free tile to select it, then click another free tile of the same type to match them.
   - Use the **Hint** button to highlight a valid move.
   - **Undo** reverts your last match.
   - **Shuffle** rearranges the remaining tiles (keeps shuffling until the board becomes solvable).
